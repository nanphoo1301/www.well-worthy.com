Objective of our website


This website is to help the living things and our environment. We make it safe, easy, and fun for anyone to give to registered charities. Want to raise money for your favourite cause? No matter how you want to give, we’re your go-to place!


Are you a registered charity? With us, you can manage and engage all your volunteer and donors in the same place. We've also introduced dashboards, where you can view and compare your giving activity with your peers.


Giving is more fun when it’s multiplied, so this is your chance to get your network involved! If you’re a little shy about your giving, we’ve got your back with options to make your profile and giving activities private, so only your members have access. If you have any more questions, you may go to FAQ to to find out more!
